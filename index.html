The Nether

To link portals from the overworld to the nether, they must be in correct locations as the Nether coordinate system is 1/8th the one of the Overworld's.

  • Place the Overworld portal (10 blocks min).
  • Press F3 to find out the coordinates.
  • Travel to the Nether and build the nether portal on the coordinates obtained from the calculator.
